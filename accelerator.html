Oswald Labs Accelerator

Program Partners Spaces Events Apply

Everything a startup needs, for 0% equity.

Oswald Labs Accelerator is a free, remote, global support program for early-stage startups building for the next billion users. The program is based out of Enschede, the Netherlands, but is open to startups worldwide.

Apply now →

How it works

We've partnered with the most innovative companies across the globe to bring you the best advice, mentorship, services, resources, and investments — all for free.

We select up to 8 startups every batch, and help them accelerate their growth for six months.

Legal Support

Hiring professional lawyers is expensive. So you don’t have to. Get free legal consultations for your startup, patent and IP support, and one-on-one meetings.

Available in the Twente region.

Office Space

Get a rented office space or access to coworking spaces in your city for free. Build your team, work unlimited hours, and have a legal office address.

Available in certain cities.

Business Development

One-on-one meetings for strategy and scalability with business development experts from around the world. Up to two hours per week.

Available worldwide.

Technology Support

Free consultations and pro-bono development from some of the leading digital studios to help improve your technology stack and user experience.

Available in the Twente region.

Research & Testing

Work with universities, NGOs, and professional user experience and testing organizations to get feedback and R&D support before you launch.

Available in London, New Delhi, and the Twente region.

And more

Free email hosting on your custom domain. Free marketing consultations. Licenses to enterprise software. There’s more from where these are from.

Available worldwide.
